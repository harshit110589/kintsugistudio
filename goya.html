GOYA

I am Abhishek, a music composer, mix engineer and founder of the New Delhi- based Kintsugi Studios. Goya is my independent music project, a space where I get to express, emote and tell a story. Although music is an auditory form of expression, for me it is very visual. I can almost always visualize a sound, a story, a photograph or a scene in my mind when I am composing. Sonically, most of my music is electronic in nature, while also combining acoustic instruments, echoes and sounds from my everyday life to create a palette that is unique and distinctly my own.
