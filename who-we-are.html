Established in 2015, we are a creative audio and music production studio, founded by Abhishek Sekhri, a music composer,producer and mix engineer with over 10 years of experience in the industry.
We believe that to be able to produce great content an artist has to be comfortable first, and we provide exactly that while maintaining a high quality of industry standard and professionalism. With a strong focus on minutest of details and aesthetics we’ve built a studio space that feels like home while maintaining an industry standard at par with the best of the studios in the world.
Some of our notable clients include brands such as Saffola, Amazon, Cred and artists such as Mickey Singh, Nav Inder, Komorebi, Kutle Khan, Samdish Bhatia, Ditty, Jordan Jonshson, Nikhil Swaroop and more.
Our expertise lies in producing music, sound design and mix and master for visual media, artists and bands. We provide audio & music related solutions such as Jingles, V.O.,Sound Design, Scoring and Mix/Master.
