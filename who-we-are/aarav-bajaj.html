aarav bajaj

Aarav is a New Delhi-based Audio Engineer and Electronic Music Producer, currently working as a recording engineer at Kintsugi Studios. He has over 3 years of experience recording for a diverse genre of artists such as Keshav Dhar, Nikhil Swaroop, Dhruv Kapoor, Mickey Singh, Ditty and Cherry Bomb.
He holds a Post Graduate Diploma in Audio Engineering & Music Production from Sri Aurobindo Centre for Arts & Communication (SACAC) and interned as an assistant Audio Engineer at Ferris Wheel Studios (Gurugram) in 2019.
His experience working with other musicians and recording artists over the years has informed and expanded his sonic palette.
