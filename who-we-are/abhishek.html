ABHISHEK sEKHRI

Abhishek is a New-Delhi based music composer, mix engineer and founder of Kintsugi Studios. His passion and love for music dates back to when he was 13 years old when he was first introduced to classics like Pink Floyd, Michael Jackson and Enigma by his Father. Having worked as a music producer for over 7 years with more than a 100 artists and bands from around the world, he consistently seeks to broaden his experience and mould his expertise in the areas of composition and mixing. In the last 7 years he has produced music and mixed for artists such as Komorebi, Mickey Singh, Navv Inder, Ditty, Easy Wanderlings, Sumit Roy, Reka Fodor, Nikhil Swaroop and Dhruv Kapoor to name a few. He has also composed for advertisements for brands such as Saffola, Amazon, Ambuja Cement and Cred. In 2018, he started his independent music project called ‘Goya’. It’s aspace where he gets to express, emote and tell a story by the use of sounds and visuals.
