aditya Dutta

Aditya is a performing multi-percussionist, music producer and audio engineer currently based in Delhi, India. He discovered his passion for music at the age of six and became a student of the Tabla under the tutelage of Pt. Shankar Ghosh, who later inducted him into the Farrukhabad Gharana. As a drummer, he has played numerous studio sessions and extensively toured the country with bands covering a wide range of genres, performing over 100 national shows. In 2016, he performed at the ICC T20 World Cup Closing Ceremony alongside Oscar nominated composer and percussionist Pt. Bickram Ghosh.After attaining a degree in Audio Engineering and Music Production, Aditya has worked on the sound design and background scores for several short films, produced music for radio and commercials and is currently working as a Music Producer, Recording and Mixing Engineer at Kintsugi Studios.
